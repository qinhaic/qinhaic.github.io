Digital CBT dramatically outperforms campus referrals for college students

《自然·人类行为》：数字CBT在大学生群体中显著优于校园转诊

📖 阅读摘要
UCLA领衔的大型随机对照试验显示，向大学生提供数字CBT的治疗接受率（74%）远超转诊至校园诊所（30%），且数字组在6周、6个月及2年随访中无症状比例均更高。该研究为高校心理健康资源配置提供了重要循证依据，发表于《自然·人类行为》5月7日刊。
A large randomized controlled trial led by UCLA published in Nature Human Behaviour (May 7) shows that offering digital CBT to college students achieves far higher treatment acceptance (74%) compared to referral to campus clinics (30%). The digital group also had higher rates of remaining symptom-free at 6-week, 6-month, and 2-year follow-ups, providing critical evidence for university mental health resource allocation.

The ACT "acceptance paradox": too much acceptance may predict more depression

《临床心理学杂志》：ACT框架下"接纳悖论"——接纳超过阈值反而预测更高抑郁

📖 阅读摘要
马萨里克大学发表在《临床心理学杂志》的纵向研究（N=344，三个时间点，每两月一次）发现，特质接纳水平超过一定阈值后，进一步升高反而预测更高的抑郁症状——这与ACT疗法"接纳越多越好"的基本假设相悖。愤怒压抑在其中起调节作用。研究者呼吁对"接纳"的适用边界进行更细致的界定。
A longitudinal study (N=344, three timepoints, bimonthly) from Masaryk University published in the Journal of Clinical Psychology found that beyond a certain threshold, higher trait acceptance actually predicted higher depressive symptoms — contradicting ACT's core assumption that "more acceptance is always better." Anger suppression served as a moderator. Researchers call for more nuanced boundaries around acceptance.
